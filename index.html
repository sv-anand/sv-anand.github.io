Biography

Dr.S.V.Anand was born in Rangoon and grew up in India. Banaras was his home town, the city is situated on the banks of the river Ganges. From his early childhood Anand showed great interest in drawing, this talent was recognized by his teachers. On his mothers advise he studied science and graduated from Agra Medical School which is situated near the famous Taj Mahal. After post graduate studies in England he emigrated to Canada in 1965 with his family and practiced surgery for many years. The passion for Arts took him to Europe where he took courses at the University of Madrid and Fontainbleu school of Arts in Paris. In subsequent years he attended several workshops guided by well known Artists in Europe. Canada and its picturesque scenaries is his inspiration, the stark changes of colour in Autumn and winter scenes are seen in many of his work. Dr. Anand has exhibited at the McMaster university with great success. In recent years he has been an Art Insturctor on cruise ships including the Queen Elizabeth during its maiden voyage around the world.
